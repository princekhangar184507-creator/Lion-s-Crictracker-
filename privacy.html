Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Lions CricTracker ("the App") is developed and operated by Lions Cricket Academy (Rangbari, Kota, Rajasthan) for use by its players, coaches, and administrators. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and how it is protected.

Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, age, gender, dominant hand, role (player/coach/admin), and contact number provided during profile setup.
  • Authentication Data: Email address used for sign-in via Firebase Authentication.
  • Profile Photo: Optional profile picture uploaded by the user.
  • Training Data: Drills completed, performance scores, XP, badges, fitness logs, and training history entered within the App.
  • Messages: Direct messages and group chat content exchanged between players, coaches, and admins within the App.
  • Usage Data: App interaction data such as login activity and feature usage, used to improve the training experience.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage player, coach, and admin profiles.
  • To track training progress, performance analytics, and provide personalised drill recommendations.
  • To enable communication between players, coaches, and academy administrators.
  • To allow coaches/admins to monitor player consistency and provide feedback.
  • To maintain academy records such as attendance, inactivity tracking, and player status.

Data Storage and Security

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore and Firebase Authentication). We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Data is only accessible to the relevant player, their coaches, and academy admins.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes. Information is only visible within the Lions Cricket Academy ecosystem (players, coaches, and admins) as required for training and academy management.

Children's Privacy

The App is used by cricket academy players, some of whom may be minors. Player profiles for minors are created and managed under the supervision of academy coaches/admins and, where applicable, with parental awareness. We do not knowingly collect more information from minors than is necessary for training and academy management purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Player data is retained as long as the player is active at the academy. Removed or retired players' data may be archived or deleted by academy administrators. Users may contact the academy to request deletion of their account and associated data.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting the academy using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
