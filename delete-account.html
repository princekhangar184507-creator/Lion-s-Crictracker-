Request Account Deletion

Lions CricTracker

If you wish to delete your Lions CricTracker account and associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Deletion

  • Contact your academy coach or admin directly through the in-app messaging system, or
  • Email the academy at the contact address below with your registered name and player ID, requesting account deletion.

Requests are processed by academy admins, since accounts are tied to academy training records. We aim to complete deletion requests within 7 business days.

What Gets Deleted

  • Profile information: name, age, gender, contact number, profile photo
  • Training data: drill history, performance scores, XP, badges, fitness logs
  • Messages sent and received within the app

What May Be Retained

  • Aggregated, non-identifiable training statistics used for academy-level analytics
  • Records required to be retained for legitimate administrative or legal purposes
